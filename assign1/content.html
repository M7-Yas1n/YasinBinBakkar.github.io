Welcome

Welcome to the wonderful world of birdwatching! We have everything you need to get started watching the birds in your local area, including tips on where to go, what equipment to use, and what to look for.

Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced birder looking to improve your skills, we have something for everyone. Our team of expert birders are passionate about sharing their knowledge with you.

So don't waste time, get outside and start watching!
